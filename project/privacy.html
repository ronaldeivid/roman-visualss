Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At roman.visualss, your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains what information I collect when you submit a project request through this website, how I use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information I Collect

When you fill out the project request form, I collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Company or brand name
  • Email address
  • Website URL
  • Project details (script status, assets, duration, deadline, description)

2. How I Use Your Information

The information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Review and respond to your project request
  • Contact you regarding next steps or follow-up questions
  • Provide an accurate quote or proposal for your project

I will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. Your data is transmitted securely and stored temporarily to deliver it to my email inbox. You can review Formspree's privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

4. Data Retention

I retain your contact information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and complete any agreed-upon project work. If no project agreement is reached, your data is not stored beyond the initial email communication.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data I hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by contacting me directly

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach me at:

bussines@romanvisualss.art

7. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.
